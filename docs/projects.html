Publications & Research

Research Areas

Quality Measurement & Improvement

Work focused on measuring and improving the quality of care for diabetes and other chronic diseases, including methods for profiling physicians and health systems.

Clinical Measurement & Precision

Research on the lack of precision in clinical measurement and its downstream impact on clinical practice and decision-making.

Methods for Prioritizing Interventions

Development and application of methods to prioritize interventions in chronic disease management and preventive services.

Methodology

  • Cluster randomized trial design and analysis
  • Quasi-experimental methods
  • Multilevel modeling in health services research
  • Clinical risk adjustment
  • Measurement theory
